Biography of Dr. John Smith

Academic Background

Dr. John Smith completed his PhD in Computer Science from Stanford University in 2010, specializing in Artificial Intelligence. Prior to that, he earned his Master's degree from the Massachusetts Institute of Technology (MIT) and his Bachelor's degree from the Indian Institute of Technology (IIT) Delhi.

Professional Experience

With over 15 years of experience in academia, Dr. Smith has published numerous papers in top-tier conferences and journals. Before joining Christ University, he worked as a research scientist at Google AI and taught at the University of California, Berkeley.

Research Interests

Dr. Smith's research focuses on deep learning architectures, natural language processing, and computer vision. His recent work explores the ethical implications of AI systems and developing more interpretable machine learning models.
